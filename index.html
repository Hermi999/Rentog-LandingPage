HOW IT WORKS

Step by Step

For Owners

Step by Step

For Renters

Create offer

Describe your equipment, set the price & maybe a deposit and upload photos. Rentog will provide you suggestions and best practices so that you will get renting requests very soon.

find & rent it

Easily find the equipment you need with our customizable search. If you've found a offer which suits your needs, you can communicate with the owner, rent the equipment and pay directly on our platform.

ship it

After the renter paid you get a notification that payment was done and that the shipping is done fully automatic and without a need of further effort from your side. We handle it.

use & return it

After you received the equipment, you can use it for the agreed time. Once the renting period is over the equipment is automatically picked up and send back to the owner by us. Once the equipment returned to the owner you get back your deposit.

get the cash

After your equipment has been picked up by our shipment partner, you receive your earnings.

OUR GOALS

Our primary goal is to establish a peer to peer sharing community for measurement equipment between companies. Since this equipment is very expensive we give the owners a chance to get a return on their investment. On the other hand we allow the renting companies to save significant costs of their usual expenses.

Here are other key factors which are very important to us:

Secure Marketplace

When you share your equipment, you don't have to worry about a thing. Our top priority is to maintain a secure marketplace and therefore we take various measures like: user verification, insurance packages, rating systems, secure payment, user communication, and much more.

Customer Satifcation

We want happy and delighted customers and we are going to ensure this by taking serveral diverent measures. One of the most important is to design the platform as easy and intuitive as possible. Another is a prompt handling and response to customer feedback.

User Convenience

Our customers do not have to take care about setting up a renting contract or handling ensurance and transport. Everything is handled by us, so that owners can focus on earing money and renter can focus on saving money and handling their projects.
